An anthology, 2023–2026

TED stands for Technology, Entertainment, Design. TEDx, the independently organized offshoot launched in 2009, was built on a single premise: that great ideas should not be confined to any one city, institution, or industry.

"The best TEDx talks are great personal essays."

I've organized and/or been part of five TEDx events over five years, working with over 105 speakers, fellow organizers, and enthusiasts. This anthology gathers 55 TEDx talks delivered between 2023 and 2026, written by speakers I've personally worked with — scientists, artists, entrepreneurs, educators, activists, and simply people with something to say.

A good TEDx talk, like a good personal essay, begins with perspective. It requires voice, argument, and structure, but above all, vulnerability. You have to be willing to tell the truth, even if that truth feels too small or too strange to matter.
