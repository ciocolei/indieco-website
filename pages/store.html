From Stage
to Screen

An anthology, 2023–2026

The most enduring ideas don't live in boardrooms or behind paywalls. They belong on stages — spoken aloud, in front of people willing to listen. Independent speaking platforms, built and run by local communities around the world, were created on a single premise: that great ideas should not be confined to any one city, institution, or industry.

"The best platform talks are great personal essays."

I've organized and/or been part of five independent speaking events over five years, working with over 105 speakers, fellow organizers, and enthusiasts. This anthology gathers 55 talks delivered between 2023 and 2026, written by speakers I've personally worked with — scientists, artists, entrepreneurs, educators, activists, and simply people with something to say.

A good platform talk, like a good personal essay, begins with perspective. It requires voice, argument, and structure, but above all, vulnerability. You have to be willing to tell the truth, even if that truth feels too small or too strange to matter.
