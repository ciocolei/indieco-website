Who we are

We are built for independent authority.

Founded in 2020, IndieCo is a PR and media advisory built for founders, executives, and experts who are ready to become the recognized name in their space. After over six years of placing clients in top-tier media, on stages, and between the covers of published books — we built the full infrastructure to do it at scale.

A book is not the goal. A keynote is not the goal. Authority is. We believe the world's most important ideas live inside people who have not yet been given the stage. Our mission is to change that — by building the infrastructure that turns expertise into influence, and influence into revenue.

Through strategic editorial thinking, a world-class speaker and media network, and a rigorous authority-building process, we take the most capable minds in any field and make them impossible to ignore.

What We Do

We don't publish books.
We engineer authority
ecosystems.

IndieCo operates across the full lifecycle of authority-building — from the first word of your book to the final stage of a global conference. Every phase of our work is designed to compound: a book becomes a media record, a media record becomes a speaker credential, and a speaker credential becomes a revenue-generating platform that outlasts any single campaign.

Our process mirrors the most rigorous startup growth frameworks — but applied to personal brand and intellectual influence. Clients who work with us don't just get placed. They get positioned. The difference is permanence.

Phase 01
Creation

Ghostwriting, developmental editing, and full-service publishing across ebook, paperback, hardcover, and audiobook — with global retail distribution in 7–8 months.

Phase 02
Bestseller Campaigns

From Amazon and Barnes & Noble entry campaigns to national placements on USA Today and the New York Times. We engineer the result — we don't hope for it.

Phase 03
Authority Expansion

Podcast interview campaigns (15–60 placements), premium media features in Forbes, Bloomberg, TechCrunch, Wall Street Journal, and global tier-one outlets.

Phase 04
Speaking & Scale

Premier conference placements, United Nations speaking engagements, signature talk development, and monetization systems that turn your book into a sustained revenue funnel.
