Who we are

A public relations and media advisory built for independent authority.

Founded in 2020, IndieCo is AI designed for media and authority. After over six years of manually publishing clients, managing casting calls and everything in between, we built a secure platform that places founders and executives in top-tier press, on marquee stages, and between the covers of their next book — across Manila and New York City.

A book is not the goal. A keynote is not the goal. Authority is. We believe the world's most important ideas live inside people who have not yet been given the stage. Our mission is to change that — by building the infrastructure that turns expertise into influence, and influence into revenue.

Through fresh strategic thinking, a world-class speaker network, and a transformative peer community, we take the most enthusiastic minds in any field and make them impossible to ignore.
